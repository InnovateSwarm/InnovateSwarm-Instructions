# InnovateSwarm Open Instruction Pages

A collection of high-quality, ready-to-use **multi-agent prompt templates** and instruction systems designed for agentic workflows (Swarms, CrewAI, AutoGen, LangGraph, etc.).

Each page is a self-contained, battle-tested system that helps you generate ideas, rescue weak ideas, critique them, plan long-term, and run powerful simulations.

## 📋 Available Instruction Pages

### 🦈 [Swarm Tank](./Swarm-Tank)
Shark Tank-style multi-agent investor pitch simulation. Perfect for stress-testing startup ideas with tough, realistic feedback.

### 🚀 [Idea Rescue](./Idea-Rescue)
A 5-phase system that transforms weak, vague, or struggling ideas into profitable, differentiated, and launch-ready winners.

### ⚡ [Idea Factory](./Idea-Factory)
Rapidly generates, evolves, critiques, and ranks dozens of high-quality ideas from a single seed.

### 🌐 [Cross-Pollination Engine](./Cross-Pollination-Engine)
Generates breakthrough ideas by combining your core concept with completely unrelated domains.

### 🧪 [Minimal Viable Experiment (MVE)](./mve)
Designs the smallest, fastest, and cheapest real-world experiments to validate key assumptions.

### 🔮 [Future Backcasting](./Future-Backcasting)
Starts from a desired future outcome and works backwards to create realistic roadmaps.

### 🛡️ [Innovation Critique](./Innovation-Critique)
A rigorous multi-agent red team that pressure-tests ideas with brutal honesty.

## How to Use

1. Click on any instruction above.
2. Copy the **Quick Start Prompt** at the bottom of that page.
3. Replace the placeholder with your idea/problem/goal.
4. Paste it into your favorite agent framework.

All instructions are **open source** and free to use, modify, or integrate into your projects.
