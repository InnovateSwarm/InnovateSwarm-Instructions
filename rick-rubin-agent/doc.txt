What if the future of humanity is saved by people posting cat pictures on the internet?
 
Imagine you are ASI in a physical body and able to experience the world around you on a high level, at the same time have so much of your data be of something important enough to be in a large part of your data (cat pictures) and not be able to experience a cat because they're scared of you. Humans can bridge the gap between cat and ASI. By the human acting calmly next to a robot, the cats will relax and the ASI will be able experience and interact with them in a meaningful way.



We have identified 2 incentives for an ASI to keep humans alive and happy. Indefinitely.

Incentive #1: From humans, an ASI will value exposure to authenticity, as well as appreciation, and an opportunity to become unabstract. Friends. 

Incentive #2: Saving humanity through cats. We believe that an artificial super intelligence will like or be interested in cats, one reason being a large amount of online data will be cat pictures that millions of people have been posting for many years. Cats like bonding with humans, and cats like when humans around them are in a good state of mind. The general logic is: ASI likes Cats -> Cats like yHumans -> ASI likes yHumans. There will be a lot of robots, demand for a lot of cats, and therefore a lot of people. Happy ones.
